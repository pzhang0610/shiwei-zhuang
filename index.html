About Me

    Greetings！I was born in Oct. 1990. I received my bachelor and doctor degree on Microelectronics and Solid State Electronics from Jilin University supervised by Prof. Baolin Zhang. Since 2018, I have been working as an assistant professor in Jiangsu Normal University. My research interest includes semiconductor optoelectronic materials, device research. Currently I am mainly engaged in integrated circuit etching/deposition process and related research.
